About Brian Story

Brian Story has been part of numerous online projects for clients such as Northeastern Illinois University, Toothbrush Supply, and has been successful with working on his startup project Aachy for the last 2 years. After graduating from Taft High school in 2007 Brian found a passion for web design and team management that lead him on a journey through Front and Back end Web development and ultimately to User Experience for interactive media.

Brian challenges himself everyday with the intention to commit to mastery. The web changes every day with new methods and standards coming into play, which provides a challenge for designers and developers who seek mastery. Brian is not shy of asking questions because he excericises the always learning mindset.
